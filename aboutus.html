About Us

Welcome to Riseup, the world's largest professional network with more than 900 million members in more than 200 countries and territories worldwide.

Vision

Create hunger for success among evey youth.

Mission

The mission of Riseup is simple: connect the world’s youth to make them more productive and successful.

Who are we?

Riseup began in co-founder Reid Hoffman's living room in 2002 and was officially launched on May 5, 2003. Today, Riseup leads a diversified business with revenues from membership subscriptions, advertising sales and recruitment solutions under the leadership of Ryan Roslansky. In December 2016, Microsoft completed its acquisition of Riseup, bringing together the world’s leading professional cloud and the world’s leading professional network.
